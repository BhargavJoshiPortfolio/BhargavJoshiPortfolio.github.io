Work

Project 1: Virtual Apartment tour with mobile VR

Project Link

This is a project to demonstarte an easy and cost effective way to use the VR tech to provide Virtual apartment tours for customers.

I have imported a simple 3D model of an apartment in Unity, used the open source "cardboard-xr-plugin" from Google to setup the mobile XR environment and placed 3 teleport zones where the user can teleport using the pointer interaction.

Project 2: Creating a Personal assistant using Artificial intelligence, a.k.a AIPA.

Project Link

This is a personal project where I use AI to create a personal assistant for myself. I use Unity, convai and C# to integrate a talking Avatar in a VR world who responds dynamically to queries about me.

I have imported a ReadyPlayerMe avatar in Unity with the help of Convai plugin. I then used the convai website to setup the character, giving him a backstory, a voice, a knowledge bank off of which the AI is trained. I then setup the UI in unity which is needed for the user to interact with the avatar.

I am working on bringing my personal projects up-to-date in order to showcase them here.

About

Welcome to my website!

My name is Bhargav Joshi, I am a 29 y/o Software developer and I currently develop Augmented and Virtual Reality applications. I did my Bachelors in Robotics and Automation from B.V.B College of Engineering and Technology in India, and later worked for a year as an Automation Engineer responsible for designing and commissioning conveyor systems. In 2018 I decided to pursue my Masters in Robotics Systems Engineering from the esteemed RWTH Aachen University in Germany, where I took courses like Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Robotics, Dynamics among other. For my Master thesis, I had the opportunity to work at Siemens to develop an AR application for an industrial use case. This is where my interest and love for the AR/VR technology developed and grew and so I went on to pursue a career in this field.

I have been working at my current company for 2.5 years now and I have had the opportunnity to work on a very unique and interesting project. My responsibilities involved but were not limited to, design and creation of AR/VR scenarios, user testing and optimisation of the scenarios, project management with git and PlasticSCM, design and creation of a week-long XR training course, organising and handling demonstrations at events, being a liason between our project partners to provide project update reports and more.
